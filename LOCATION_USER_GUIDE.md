# Location Tracking - User Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Grant Location Permission

1. Navigate to the **Location Tracking** screen
2. Tap **"Start Tracking"**
3. When prompted, **allow** location permission
4. The app will ask for both foreground and background location access

### Step 2: View Your Location

- Tap **"Get Current Location"** to see your current coordinates
- You'll see:
  - **Latitude** (7 decimal places)
  - **Longitude** (7 decimal places)
  - **Accuracy** (in meters - lower is better)
  - **Last updated time**

### Step 3: Start Tracking

1. Tap **"Start Tracking"** button
2. The app will begin recording your location every 10 seconds
3. You'll see a **green indicator** (● Tracking Active)
4. Your coordinates will update automatically
5. All locations are saved to your device

## Common Tasks

### How to Pause Tracking (Without Stopping)

1. While tracking, tap **"Pause Tracking"**
2. The app will show an **orange indicator** (● Tracking Paused)
3. Locations will stop being recorded
4. Your current location is still shown but frozen
5. Tap **"Resume Tracking"** to continue recording

**Use this when**: You want to temporarily stop recording but keep the session active

### How to Stop Tracking Completely

1. Tap **"Stop Tracking"**
2. Recording stops
3. Background tracking stops
4. Current location display clears
5. Your location history is **preserved** - not deleted
6. All previously recorded locations remain saved

**Use this when**: You want to end the tracking session entirely

### How to View Your Location History

1. Scroll down to **Location History** section
2. See your last 10 recorded locations with:
   - Location number
   - Coordinates
   - Accuracy
   - Date and time
3. The total number of locations recorded is shown at the top

### How to Delete All Location Data

1. Scroll down to **Location History**
2. Tap **"Clear All"** button
3. Confirm deletion in the popup
4. All your recorded locations will be permanently deleted

## Tips for Best Results

### GPS Tips

- **Outdoors**: GPS works best outside with clear sky view
- **Indoors**: GPS accuracy is reduced inside buildings
- **Urban**: Tall buildings can affect GPS signal
- **Movement**: Walk or drive to generate good tracking data

### For Accurate Location

1. Tap "Get Current Location" for highest accuracy
2. Wait a few seconds for GPS lock
3. Accuracy in meters shows how precise the location is
4. Smaller numbers = better accuracy

### Battery Tips

- **Active Tracking**: Uses ~5-10% battery per hour
- **Long Sessions**: Plug in your device
- **Pause When Idle**: Use pause feature to save battery between recording periods

## Troubleshooting

### "Location Permission Required" Message

**What it means**: The app needs permission to access your location  
**How to fix**:

1. Check your device Settings
2. Go to Location or Permissions
3. Find this app and allow location access
4. Return to app and try again

### Coordinates Not Updating

**What it means**: GPS isn't getting a signal  
**How to fix**:

1. Go outside (indoors signal is weak)
2. Look at the sky (remove obstructions)
3. Wait 10-20 seconds for GPS lock
4. Try again

### App Says Tracking Started But No Updates

**What it means**: Background permissions might not be granted  
**How to fix**:

1. Check Settings → Permissions
2. Ensure location permission is set to "Always" or "Allow all the time"
3. Some devices also have battery optimization - disable for this app

### Can't Find Previous Locations

**What it means**: You may have cleared the data  
**How to fix**:

1. Once data is deleted via "Clear All", it cannot be recovered
2. In the future, use "Pause Tracking" to save battery instead of stopping

## FAQ

**Q: Where is my location data stored?**  
A: Only on your device, in the app's local storage. Never sent anywhere.

**Q: Can I see my data on a map?**  
A: Currently, you see coordinates in a list. Map view may be added in future updates.

**Q: What if I close the app while tracking?**  
A: No problem! Background tracking continues. When you reopen the app, previous locations are still there.

**Q: How accurate is the GPS?**  
A: Typically 5-30 meters in good conditions. Accuracy improves outdoors with clear sky.

**Q: Can I share my location data?**  
A: Currently the data stays on your device only. You can note coordinates manually if needed.

**Q: Does this drain my battery?**  
A: Yes, but not excessively. Continuous GPS tracking uses ~5-10% battery per hour. Similar to GPS maps/navigation.

**Q: Can I recover deleted locations?**  
A: No. Once deleted via "Clear All", data cannot be recovered. Be cautious.

**Q: Does the app need internet?**  
A: No. Everything works offline, entirely on your device.

**Q: How many locations can I record?**  
A: As many as your device storage allows. Typically thousands without issues.
